Chicken Tagine

Description

What I'm making for dinner today

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast
  • 1 tbsp ras el hanout
  • 1 red onion
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 stick cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp harissa
  • 2 preserved lemons
  • 1 tin (450g) chopped tomatoes
  • 100g dried apricots
  • fresh herbs (coriander, parsley, mint)
  • olive oil
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Sweat down the onion, garlic, ras el hanout, and cinnamon.
  2. Once onions are soft, stir in the harissa. Add the diced chicken and quickly brown on all sides.
  3. Half the preserved lemon and scrape out the pulp. Finely slice the peel and add it to the chicken, alongside the tomatoes and apricots.
  4. Cut a cartouche and lay over the mixture, put the lid on. Simmer for 30-40 minutes. Add some stock if it starts to dry out.
  5. Season, garnish with herbs, and serve alongside grains.
