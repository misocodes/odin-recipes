Tepache

Description

Refreshing fermented beverage for an uncharacteristically hot day

Ingredients

  • 800g pinepapple peel and core
  • 250g unrefined cane sugar or brown sugar
  • 2.5 liters water
  • 20g ginger, sliced, optional
  • cinnamon stick
  • 3L Kilner Fermentation Jar

Steps

  1. Dissolve the sugar in the water and stir well.
  2. Add all the ingredients to the vessel, weighing it down, and securing the lid with fermentation pipe.
  3. Ferment with 1-3 days, depending on the room temperature. See if there is white foam at regular intervals, removing it before consuming.
  4. Bottle in flip top jars to seal in bubbles, though burp it once a day.
