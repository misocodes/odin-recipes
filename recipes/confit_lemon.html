Confit Lemon

Description

Beautifully sweet, sour, and bitter conserve for dressing up sweet or savoury dishes

Ingredients

  • 500g lemon
  • 50g cane sugar
  • 500ml water

Steps

  1. Cut the lemon into chunks with the skin on, removing extra pith and seeds.
  2. Dissolve the sugar in the water and add the lemon. Cook on medium until the lemon is soft and translucent.
  3. Strain the syrup and reserve in a Kilner jar. Finely chop the cooked lemon and add back into the syrup.
